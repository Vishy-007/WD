Dogs/h1>

Dogs are domesticated mammals that have been companions to humans for thousands of years. They are known for their loyalty, playfulness, and diverse personalities, with hundreds of breeds exhibiting a wide range of sizes, appearances, and temperaments. Beyond being beloved pets, dogs also serve as working animals, assisting in tasks like herding, hunting, and providing assistance to those with disabilities

Dogs exhibit a range of behaviors, from playful and affectionate to protective and even territorial. Their keen senses of smell and hearing make them valuable for tracking and detection. Historically, dogs were primarily used for hunting and guarding, but today, they are cherished members of countless families, offering companionship, emotional support, and a unique bond with their human counterparts
